Quick answers

How accurate is this estimate?

Within about ±15% for most homes. We confirm a firm price before any work begins — no surprises.

Inside & outside or just outside?

Use the "Both sides" / "Outside only" toggle on each row. Outside-only is roughly 60% of the both-sides price.

What counts as a 2nd-story window?

Anything that needs a ladder past first-floor reach. Flat $3 per window on top of the regular size price.

Do you charge for hard water or heavy build-up?

Light spotting is +$4 per window, heavy mineral build-up is +$8. We'll confirm with you before adding it to the bill.

What's the service area?

The whole Flathead Valley — Kalispell, Whitefish, Columbia Falls, Bigfork, Lakeside, Somers. Beyond 25 mi from Kalispell may have a small travel fee.
