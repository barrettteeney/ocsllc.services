01What we do

Every pane, done by hand.

Four ways we work — one standard. Hand-detailed glass, careful prep, and a walk-through before we leave.

Residential

Your home, spotless.

More than spraying glass and walking away — we hand-detail every pane, work the corners where dust collects, vacuum out tracks, and wipe down the sills. Glass you can actually see through.

  • Interior glass cleaned by hand, edge to edge
  • Exterior glass with a professional squeegee technique
  • Screens removed, hand-washed, and replaced
  • Sills wiped, tracks vacuumed and detailed
  • Drop cloths and shoe covers inside your home
  • Walk-through with you before we leave
Price my home →
Post-Construction

Builder-ready glass.

Fresh-build glass comes covered in stucco bleed, paint overspray, drywall dust, and sticker adhesive. The wrong tools leave permanent scratches — we use the right ones, pane by pane.

  • Stucco, mortar splatter, and paint overspray removed
  • Manufacturer labels and sticker adhesive stripped
  • Razor detailing on standard glass, plastic scrapers on low-E and coated glass
  • Sills, tracks, and frames detailed after the glass is clear
  • Final walk-through with the GC or project manager
Quote my build →
Commercial

Storefronts that shine.

Customers judge a business before they step inside — dirty glass is one of the loudest signals something’s off. We keep storefronts, offices, and multi-tenant buildings sharp, on a schedule built around your hours.

  • Exterior storefront glass, hand-detailed
  • Recurring routes — weekly, monthly, or quarterly
  • After-hours and early-morning service available
  • Certificate of insurance on request for property managers
  • Flat per-visit pricing, invoiced your way
