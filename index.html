Window cleaning in Kalispell & the Flathead Valley

Interior and exterior glass, screens, and tracks — cleaned by hand and finished streak-free. Locally owned and insured, serving Kalispell, Whitefish, Columbia Falls, Bigfork, and the wider valley.

Interior & exterior glass

Inside and outside of every pane, hand-cleaned with a squeegee and scrubber. We use a water-fed pole only where ladders aren't safe, so even high second-story glass comes out clear.

Screens cleaned

Screens trap pollen, dust, and valley wildfire smoke. We clean them on request so they stop dulling your view and don't redeposit grime on freshly cleaned glass.

Tracks & sills detailed

We wipe the frames and detail the tracks and sills, clearing out the dirt and dead bugs that collect there — the finishing touch most cleaners skip.

Hard water & build-up

Sprinkler overspray and mineral staining are common on Flathead Valley windows. We inspect for hard water staining and treat it so the glass actually looks clean, not just wet.

Window cleaning FAQ

How much does window cleaning cost in Kalispell?

Most homes land between $150 and $400 depending on size, number of panes, and whether you add interior cleaning, screens, or tracks. There is a $150 service minimum. You see your personalized price range as soon as you pick a booking time — no waiting on a callback.

Do you clean both interior and exterior windows?

Yes. You can choose interior + exterior (inside and outside glass, plus frames and tracks wiped) or exterior-only. Screens and tracks can be added to either option.

Do you clean window screens and tracks?

Yes. Screens are cleaned on request, and window tracks and sills are detailed so you don't see grime when the morning sun comes through the glass.

What areas do you serve?

Kalispell, Whitefish, Columbia Falls, Bigfork, Lakeside, and the wider Flathead Valley. Travel anywhere in the valley is free — there is no trip surcharge.

Is OCS LLC insured?

Yes. OCS LLC carries general liability insurance and is locally owned and operated by Barrett Teeney. You talk to the same person who shows up to do the work.

How soon can you clean my windows?

Most quotes come back the same day, and we typically schedule one to two weeks out depending on the season. You can lock in a time instantly through the booking form.

Do you offer a guarantee?

Yes. Every job comes with a 48-hour streak-free guarantee. If anything looks off within 48 hours, we come back and make it right.
