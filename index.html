Who We Are

At OCS LLC, we're not just here to clean windows — we're here to make your space feel cleaner, clearer, and taken care of. Based in Kalispell and serving the Flathead Valley, we specialize in both residential and commercial window cleaning with a focus on quality, consistency, and personable relationships with our customers.

We treat every project like it matters — because to you, it does. Whether residential or a local business, we approach each job with care and attention to detail. We're not about shortcuts or surface-level service. We're about showing up, doing it right the first time, and making sure you're genuinely satisfied.

"Work Done Well, Work Done Right." That’s not just our motto — it’s how we operate.
