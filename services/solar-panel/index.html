Why dirty panels lose money

A coating of dust, pollen, bird droppings, or wildfire ash can cut solar output 5–25%. We hand-rinse with purified water and soft cloths — no abrasive brushes, no harsh chemicals, no risk to the panel surface or warranty.

What's included

  • Roof-access setup with safety harness when needed
  • Soft brush + purified water rinse, panel-by-panel
  • Frame and rail wipe-down
  • Visible debris removal (leaves, twigs, nests if abandoned)
  • Photos before/after on request
