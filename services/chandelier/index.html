What we clean

  • Foyer and great-room chandeliers, vaulted-ceiling fixtures
  • High clerestory windows that ladders or step stools won't reach
  • Recessed and pendant fixture glass
  • Stairwell-cathedral windows that span two stories interior
  • Custom commercial fixtures (hospitality, churches, civic spaces)

How we work

We use stable platform ladders or pole-mounted soft-microfiber tools depending on the fixture. We hand-clean to avoid the streaks and chemical residue that pole-only methods leave on crystal and prismatic glass. Cloth drops protect floors and furniture below.

Pricing

Quoted per fixture based on size, height, and crystal count. Often bundles with annual window cleaning. See pricing →
