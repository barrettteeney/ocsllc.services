What's included

  • Safe roof access via ladder or pure-water pole when terrain allows
  • Hand-detail squeegee or scrubber pass per skylight
  • Frame & flashing wipe-down
  • Pollen, sap, and tree debris removal

Pricing

Skylights are priced as Large windows ($14 one-side, ladder included) and bundle well with full-home cleans. Bigger skylights (over 4×6 ft) priced individually.

Safety note

We don't access roofs that are unsafe — pitch over 8/12, snow/ice cover, or visible damage. In those cases we'll quote pure-water pole work from below where possible.
