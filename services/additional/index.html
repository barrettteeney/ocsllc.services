Window cleaning is our core, but we hand-detail anything else made of glass or solar cells too. Most of these bundle well with a standard window cleaning visit so you only pay one trip charge.

Solar panel cleaning

A coating of dust, pollen, bird droppings, or wildfire ash can cut solar output 5–25%. We hand-rinse with purified water and soft cloths — no abrasive brushes, no harsh chemicals, no warranty risk.

  • Roof-access setup with safety harness when needed
  • Soft brush + purified water rinse, panel-by-panel
  • Frame and rail wipe-down
  • Visible debris removal (leaves, twigs, abandoned nests)

Skylights

Roof-access skylight cleaning, hand-detailed. Often included automatically when accessible during a full-home visit.

  • Safe roof access via ladder or pure-water pole when terrain allows
  • Hand-detail squeegee or scrubber pass per skylight
  • Frame & flashing wipe-down
  • Pollen, sap, and tree debris removal

We don't access roofs that are unsafe — pitch over 8/12, snow/ice cover, or visible damage. In those cases we'll quote pole work from below where possible.

Chandeliers & hard-to-reach interior glass

Vaulted ceilings, mountain-home great rooms, statement fixtures. Hand-detailed.

  • Foyer and great-room chandeliers, vaulted-ceiling fixtures
  • High clerestory windows ladders or step stools won't reach
  • Recessed and pendant fixture glass
  • Stairwell-cathedral windows that span two stories interior
  • Custom commercial fixtures (hospitality, churches, civic spaces)

Frames, tracks & sills

Wiped down on every window cleaning job — no extra charge. We don't deep-clean tracks (that's a separate service if requested), but they leave looking clean and dust-free.
