What we charge for

The glass

Every pane, inside and out. Bundle saves you money.

Hard water / build-up

Mineral spotting takes longer to remove. Quoted on-site if needed.

Post-construction

Stucco, paint, debris from new builds. Small per-pane add-on.

Travel beyond 25 mi

If a job is further than 25 miles from Kalispell, a small travel fee may apply.

What we DON'T charge for

  • Estimates - free, in person or by photo
  • Travel within the Flathead Valley (within 25 mi of Kalispell)
  • Going back up if we missed a streak (48-hour satisfaction guarantee)
  • Drop cloths, shoe covers, supplies
  • Weather reschedules
