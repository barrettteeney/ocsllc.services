Quick reference: typical price ranges

Every job is quoted before we start. These are the ranges most jobs fall in for the Flathead Valley. Smaller homes and storefronts trend toward the lower end; larger homes, three-story builds, and lake-view properties trend higher. You'll always get a firm price before we start work.

Residential window cleaning

$200–$500
typical home
  • Interior + exterior glass
  • Screens hand-washed
  • Sills and tracks detailed
  • Drop cloths inside
  • Walk-through at the end

Smaller cottages or condos can run $150–$200. Lake homes or 3-story properties can run $500–$1,000+.

Post-construction

$300–$1,500
per project
  • Stucco and paint overspray
  • Manufacturer label removal
  • Drywall dust and debris
  • Sills, tracks, surrounding finishes
  • GC walk-through

Priced per pane count + complexity. Custom homes with high vaulted glass are at the upper end.

Pressure washing

$200–$600
residential
  • Driveways, patios, sidewalks
  • Decks (low-pressure soft-wash)
  • Siding (vinyl, metal, fiber-cement)
  • Fences and brick

Bundle with window cleaning and we typically discount the combined rate by 10-20%.

Commercial and recurring

Storefronts, offices, restaurants, and multi-tenant buildings on a recurring schedule are priced per visit at a flat rate. Recurring service is usually 15–30% cheaper per visit than one-off cleans because we can schedule efficiently.

  • Small storefront, weekly: $40–$80 per visit
  • Mid-size office, monthly: $150–$300 per visit
  • Multi-tenant or 3-story commercial: custom quote

Net-15 or net-30 invoicing available, COI on request, after-hours service available — just ask.

What affects the final price

Pane count + size

More panes = more time. Big picture windows count as one pane but take longer than four small ones.

Number of stories

Single-story is straightforward. Two-story takes ladders or water-fed pole. Three-story takes more time and equipment.

Interior vs. exterior

Exterior-only is cheaper than both. Interior takes drop cloths, careful furniture work, and slower passes.

Screen condition

Standard hand-washing is included. Heavily caked screens or torn frames may need extra time or replacement.

Hard-water staining

Light spots come out with our standard process. Heavy mineral buildup needs specialty treatment, quoted separately.

Travel

No drive-time fees within Kalispell, Whitefish, Columbia Falls, or Bigfork. Outlying areas are priced case-by-case.

What we don't charge for

  • Estimates — always free, in person or by photo
  • Travel within the Flathead Valley
  • "Going back up" if we missed a streak — we just fix it
  • Drop cloths, shoe covers, or any standard supplies
  • Last-minute reschedules due to weather (we reschedule the day-of if it's actively raining or freezing)
