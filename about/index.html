Hi — I'm Barrett.

I started OCS LLC because I wanted to run the kind of company I'd want to hire myself. One that picks up the phone. One that quotes a fair price and sticks to it. One that shows up when it says it will, does the job carefully, and doesn't try to upsell you on things you don't need.

Window cleaning is a craft, and pressure washing is a craft, and I take both seriously. The difference between a streak-free pane and a half-finished one is patience, the right tools, and giving a damn. Same for a driveway that comes back to color or a deck that doesn't get etched. I work on every job personally — when you call OCS, you're talking to the person on the ladder.
