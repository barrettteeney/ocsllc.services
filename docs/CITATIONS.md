# OCS LLC — Local Citations Playbook

These are the local directories every Flathead Valley service business should be listed in. Each citation is another signal to Google that OCS LLC is a real, locally-relevant business — and several drive direct calls/leads on their own. They also act as defensive signals: if a competitor lists OCS LLC inaccurately, your own citations override the bad data.

**Submit each one with the EXACT same NAP block below.** Consistency matters more than completeness — Google penalizes mismatched name/address/phone across listings.

---

## Master NAP block — copy/paste this everywhere

```
Name:        OCS LLC
Legal name:  Opus Cordis Services LLC
Phone:       (406) 607-2151
Email:       barrett@ocsllc.services
Website:     https://ocsllc.services
Address:     Kalispell, MT 59901
Service area: Kalispell, Whitefish, Columbia Falls, Bigfork, Flathead Valley
Categories:  Window cleaning service, Pressure washing service
Hours:       (use whatever you have set on Google Business Profile)
Description: Professional residential, commercial, and post-construction
             window cleaning and pressure washing serving the Flathead Valley.
             Licensed, insured, and 5-star reviewed.
Year est.:   (your start year)
Owner:       Barrett Teeney
```

**Service-area business note:** Don't publish a street address publicly. For "address required" forms, list Kalispell, MT 59901 only and check the "service area" / "I do not have a physical storefront" box if available.

## After-job review request — text + email templates

Send within 24 hours of finishing a job, while the customer is still impressed.

### SMS template (best response rate)

```
Hi [first name] — Barrett with OCS LLC. Thanks again for letting us
clean your windows today. If we did right by you, the kindest thing
you could do is leave us a 30-second Google review:
👉 [your Google review short link]
Either way, thanks for the business — let me know any time.
```

### Email template

```
Subject: Thanks again, [first name]

Hi [first name],

Thanks again for choosing OCS LLC. The crew said your place was great
to work on — appreciate you having us out.

If you're happy with the result, the single most helpful thing you
can do for a small local business like ours is leave a quick Google
review. Doesn't have to be long — even one sentence makes a big
difference.

  → [your Google review short link]

If anything wasn't perfect, please reply to this email and tell me
directly so I can fix it. Either way, thanks for the trust.

— Barrett
OCS LLC
(406) 607-2151
ocsllc.services
```

### Get your Google review short link

1. Open your Google Business Profile dashboard.
2. Find the "Get more reviews" card or "Share review form" option.
3. Copy the short URL — looks like `g.page/r/...` or `bit.ly/...`.
4. Paste into the templates above.

## What I'd do, in order

If you only do five things from this doc:

1. **Bing Places** (15 min) — exact same fields as GBP. Just sign in, paste, done.
2. **Apple Business Connect** (15 min) — same. Both unlock major maps platforms outside Google.
3. **Nextdoor** (10 min) — high signal in your specific market.
4. **Set up the SMS review-request workflow** — even sending it manually after each job will compound over months.
5. **Bigfork or Whitefish Chamber membership** — pick whichever serves more of your target customer mix. Annual cost, big trust boost.

That's about 90 minutes of work and probably the best 90 minutes you'll spend on local SEO this year.
