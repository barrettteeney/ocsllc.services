Residential Window Cleaning for Kalispell homes and businesses

Kalispell sits at the heart of the Flathead Valley with the busiest mix of homes, businesses, and new construction. Properties here range from older homes around Main Street to new builds out toward Lone Pine, Whitefish Stage, and the airport corridor. Residential jobs here usually mean twice a year (spring and fall), pre-listing prep, post-move-in.

What it costs: Most Kalispell homes fall between $200 and $500 depending on pane count and stories. We give a firm price up front — no upcharges on the day of service.

  • Exterior glass cleaned with a professional squeegee technique
  • Screens removed, hand-washed, and replaced
  • Sills wiped down — wood, metal, or vinyl
  • Tracks vacuumed and detailed
  • Drop cloths and shoe covers used inside your home
  • Walk-through with you at the end
