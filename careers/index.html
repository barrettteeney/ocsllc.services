Why work with us

  • Real craft. We hand-clean for the luxury feel — squeegee, scrubber, attention to detail. No water-fed-pole-only shortcuts.
  • Fair pay. Hourly + tips on residential, commission options on commercial. We pay weekly.
  • Full training. No experience needed. We'll teach you the squeegee, ladder safety, customer interaction, every step.
  • Best office in the world. Whitefish lake homes, Big Mountain, downtown Kalispell. You're exterior, you're moving, you're making something look brand new every day.
  • Eco-friendly products. Pet-safe, plant-safe, biodegradable.

What we look for

  • Comfortable on a ladder up to 2nd story
  • Customer-friendly, presents well at someone's home
  • Reliable, on-time, no-drama
  • Driver's license + reliable transportation a plus
  • Comfortable working in all weather (yes, including winter)

How to apply

This isn't a corporate process. Send us a quick text or email — tell us your name, where you're from, anything you've done that's similar (cleaning, construction, hospitality, restaurant, anything customer-facing), and when you're available. We'll get back to you fast.
