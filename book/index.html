Quick FAQ

Why do I have to pick a home size instead of typing my square footage exactly?

The online calendar can’t resize a time block on the fly, so I bracket homes into four standard sizes. Pick the bracket your home falls into — if it’s on the border, pick the larger one. I’ll adjust the time block if needed when I confirm your quote.

What if I don’t know how many windows I have?

Skip that field. Square footage is enough to estimate. Window count is a sanity check for when houses have an unusual amount of glass (lots of picture windows, lake-facing builds, etc.).

How far ahead can I book?

Today through September 30, 2026. Minimum 24 hours of lead time so I can confirm your quote before you expect me.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes, free up to 24 hours before. After that, call or text me at (406) 607-2151 and we’ll figure it out — I’m reasonable.

Do you do commercial?

Yes. Storefronts, offices, multi-story buildings, recurring monthly contracts. Those are easier to quote in person or by photo — give me a call.
